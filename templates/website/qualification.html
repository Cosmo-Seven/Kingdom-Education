Vocational Qualification in Cleaning and Property Services

Are you looking for a stable, in-demand career with excellent employment prospects in Finland and across the EU? Kingdom Education Mission Euro ry (KEME) invites international students to embark on a high-quality vocational education journey to become a certified professional in Cleaning and Property Services. We bridge the gap, bringing the excellence of Finnish vocational training to you.

Why Choose Cleaning and Property Services in Finland?

Finland is renowned for its high standards in maintenance, cleanliness, and sustainable practices. Professionals in this sector are vital, ensuring safe, healthy, and functional environments across various industries. With a strong demand for skilled workers, this qualification offers immediate employability, competitive salaries, and clear pathways for career growth and long-term residency in Finland. KEME works closely with leading Finnish vocational institutions to deliver a program that is not just education, but a stepping stone to a successful future.

Program Structure: Modules Designed for Success

I.Common Units (35 competence points)

These transversal skills are mandatory for all vocational qualifications in Finland, preparing you for success in anyprofessional environment

  • Communication & Interaction

II. Vocational Units (145 competence points)

1. Compulsory Unit (15 competence points): Production of Customer-Oriented Cleaning and Property Services Learn to interpret service agreements, plan tasks efficiently, select appropriate methods and equipment, ensure quality and safety, and document your work while always prioritizing customer satisfaction.

2.Compulsory Vocational Units within a Chosen Competence Area (typically 60 competence points) You will specialize in one of the following key areas: Competence Area of Site Facilities Services (Site Facilities Operative): Focus on comprehensive maintenance and basic cleaning in non-residential settings like offices, schools, and commercial spaces. You'll master professional techniques, machinery operation, specialized surface care, waste management, and customer service in institutional environments. Competence Area of Domestic Services (Housekeeper): Specialization in household cleaning, home maintenance, and support services for private residences or individuals with special needs. Competence Area of Property Maintenance: Broader facility upkeep, including minor repairs, outdoor areas, and integrated property services.

3. Optional Units (Remaining competence points, e.g., 70 competence points) Customize your expertise with electives such as: Deep cleaning and specialized services (e.g., healthcare, food industry) Advanced machine handling Enhanced sustainability practices Additional competence areas for broader skill development Assessment: Your skills will be evaluated through demonstrated professional performance in real or simulated work environments, portfolio development, and workplace evaluations, ensuring you meet Finland's high competence-based standards
