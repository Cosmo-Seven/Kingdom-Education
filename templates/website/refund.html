Kingdom Education Mission Euro Ry

At Kingdom Education Mission Euro Ry, we are committed to transparency and fairness when processing refund requests. This policy explains how our refund claim form works, what information is required, and how we handle submissions.

The Refund Claim Form is our official tool for collecting the information needed to process your refund request. Using this form ensures that:

  • We receive all necessary details to process your request efficiently
  • Your request is handled consistently and fairly
  • We have proper documentation for our records and for any third-party institutions (such as schools or universities)

The Refund Claim Form is our official tool for collecting the information needed to process your refund request. Using this form ensures that:

Situation Form Required
Refund for a KEME program or event Yes
Refund for school fees paid through KEME Yes
Refund for university fees paid through KEME Yes
Dispute regarding a payment Yes
Request to transfer a payment to another program Contact us directly

Note: Donations are generally non-refundable and do not require a claim form unless exceptional circumstances apply.

To process your refund request without delay, please ensure all relevant sections of the form are completed accurately.

3.1 Mandatory Information
Field Requirement
Full Name Must match the name used during registration or payment
Contact Information Valid email and phone number for updates
Program /Institution Name Clearly identify what the payment was for
Payment Amount Exact amount paid
Payment Date When the payment was made
Reason for Refund Select the appropriate reason and provide details

3.2 Supporting Documents

You must attach the following documents where applicable:

  • Payment confirmation or receipt (required for all refunds)
  • Withdrawal confirmation (for school or university fee refunds)
  • Official correspondence from the institution (if applicable)
  • Medical certificate (if requesting a refund due to illness or emergency)

Incomplete submissions may result in processing delays or denial of the request.

Review of request (school/university fees) Dependent on institution’s response
Refund processing (if approved) 30–45 business days for KEME programs
Final notification Within 3 business days of decision

6.1 Grounds for Approval

A refund request may be approved if:

  • The request is submitted in accordance with the applicable refund policy (KEME Terms or institution’s policy)
  • All required information and supporting documents are provided
  • The request falls within the permitted cancellation or refund window
  • The program was cancelled by KEME
6.2 Grounds for Denial

A refund request may be denied if:

  • The request is submitted after the cancellation deadline
  • Required supporting documents are missing or incomplete
  • The refund does not align with the institution’s refund policy (for school/university fees)
  • The request is for a non-refundable donation or administrative fee
  • The participant was removed from a program due to misconduct

If your request is denied, we will provide a written explanation and, where possible, guidance on next steps.

If your refund request is denied, you may appeal the decision by following these steps:

  1. Contact us within 14 days of receiving the denial notice
  2. Provide additional information or documentation that supports your appeal
  3. Submit your appeal in writing to info@keme.fi with the subject line: Appeal – Refund Denial – [Your Name]

Appeals will be reviewed by a designated staff member not involved in the original decision. You will receive a final decision within 14 business days.

All information submitted through the Refund Claim Form is processed in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and our Privacy Policy. Your data is used solely for:

  • Processing your refund request
  • Communicating with you regarding your request
  • Liaising with educational institutions when applicable
  • Maintaining accurate financial records

We do not share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to process your refund (e.g., with schools or universities) or as required by law.
